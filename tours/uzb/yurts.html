Uzbekistan adventure with yurts - 12 days tour program
Rating:
Duration: 12 days
Route: Tashkent-Khiva-Bukhara-Yangikazgan-Sentyab Village-Samarkand-Tashkent-Kokand-Rishtan-Margilan-Ferghana-Tashkent
Uzbekistan adventure with yurts - 12 days tour program
Tashkent-Khiva-Bukhara-Yangikazgan-Sentyab Village-Samarkand-Tashkent-Kokand-Rishtan-Margilan-Ferghana-Tashkent

Arrival of the clients at Tashkent International Airport by TK-368 flight from Istanbul at 06:35am. Meet and transfer to hotel (early check-in after 07:00am is included). PM. Half day city tour of Tashkent. Visit Khastimom complex (Kaffal Shashiy Masoleum, Barak Khan Medressah, Library with holy Koran), Kukaldosh Medressah, Chorsu Bazaar, Applied Arts Museum. Overnight at Hotel.

Meals: Breakfast at Hotel.

After early breakfast transfer to airport for morning HY-1051 (07:50-09:30) flight to Urgench. Meet and transfer to Khiva (Check-in after 14:00). Upon arrival start full day city tour of Ichankala (inner town). Visit Kalta Minar, Muhammad Aminkhan Medressah, Kunya Ark, Muhammad Rakhimkhan Medressah, Juma Mosque and Minaret, Pahlavan Mahmud Masoleum, Islamkhodja Medressah, Toshhovli Palace, Allakulikhan Medressah and Ceravanserai. Overnight at Hotel.

Meals: Early breakfast at Hotel.

After breakfast drive to Khiva via Kizilkum desert. En route photo stop at Amu Darya river bank. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast full day city tour of Bukhara. Visit Samanid’s Mausoleum, Chashma Ayub, citadel Ark, Bolo-Hauz Mosque, Kalyan complex including Kalyan Mosque, Kalyan Minaret and Mirarab Medressah, Trade domes, Abdulazizkhan and Ulugbek Medressahs, Nadir Devonbegi Medressah, complex, Lyabi-Khauz complex. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast continue exploring Bukhara. Visit Sitorai Mohi Hosa – summer residence of Bukhara emirs, Chor Minor Mosque, Chor Bakr mausoleum, Naqshbandiy mausoleum. PM. Free time. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast drive to Gijduvan and visit ceramist family. Then drive to Navoi, en route visit the ruins of Rabbat Malik Caravansaray. Then drive to Nurata, a small town near Aydarkul Lake. This place is famous for holy spring “Chashmai Ayub”. According to the old traditions the Alexander the great built a small fortress to watch the enemy from the Northern part: here you can visit ruins of two old castles which dating back to unknown period. Also visit Nurata mosque from eight century. Upon arrival in Yangikazgan, short camel riding within yurt camp (about 10 minutes). Overnight in yurts.

After breakfast visit Aydarkul lake, then drive to Sentyab/ Asraf village. Upon arrival guided tour through the village. See building style of the local houses, and watch the traditional livelihood activities of the mountain dwellers, explore the Kardvan valley. Then relax on the tapchan or supa under the walnut trees. Learn livelihood and land use activities. Help or watch to prepare traditional dish. Overnight at local guesthouse.

Meals: Breakfast at Yurts. Lunch at guesthouse. Dinner at guesthouse.

After breakfast drive to Samarkand. On arrival proceed to city sightseeing. Visit Bibihanum Mosque, bazaar, Ulugbek Observatory. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast full day city tour of Samarkand. Visit Gur Emir Mausoleum, Reghistan Square (Ulugbek Madrasah, Sher Dor Madrasah and Tilla Kari Madrasah), Afrosiyob Museum, Shahi Zinda necropolis. In the afternoon transfer to the train station for Afrosiyob express train to Tashkent (17:00-19:10 or 18:00-20:10). Overnight at hotel.

After breakfast take morning train (08:05-12:05) to Kokand, one of the ancient cities of Uzbekistan located in the western part of Fergana valley. Kokand was the capital of Kokand Khanate from 1709-1876, the territory of which included big part of present Uzbekistan, part of Southern Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan and Southern China. Kokand considered being the second religious center after Bukhara in Central Asia: there were 35 madrassah and hundreds of mosques in Kokand city which were mainly built during the reign of Khudoyar Khan. In Kokand visit famous Khudoyar Khan’s Palace (closed Monday), Juma Mosque and Dahmai Shahon (Grave of Kings). Drive to Rishtan to visit ceramist workshop. Then drive to Fergana. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast drive to Margilan and visit Silk factory “Yodgorlik”, bazaar, Chakar Mosque, Said Akhmad Khodja Madrassah. Then drive back to Tashkent. Overnight at Hotel.

Transfer to airport for departure flight to Istanbul.
