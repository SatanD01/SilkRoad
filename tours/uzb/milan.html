7-day Uzbekistan tour with HY flights from Milan
Rating:
Duration: 7 days
Route: Urgench-Khiva-Bukhara-Shakhrisabz-Samarkand-Tashkent
7-day Uzbekistan tour with HY flights from Milan
Urgench-Khiva-Bukhara-Shakhrisabz-Samarkand-Tashkent

Arrival of the clients at Urgench International Airport by HY-255 flight from Milan at 06:35. Meet and transfer to Khiva (Check-in after 12:00). Upon arrival start full day city tour of Ichankala (inner town). Visit Kalta Minar, Muhammad Aminkhan Medressah, Kunya Ark, Muhammad Rakhimkhan Medressah, Juma Mosque and Minaret, Pahlavan Mahmud Masoleum, Islamkhodja Medressah, Toshhovli Palace, Allakulikhan Medressah and Ceravanserai. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast drive to Khiva via Kizilkum desert. En route photo stop at Amu Darya river bank. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast transfer to Urgench airport for HY-1052 (10:10-11:10) flight to Bukhara. Upon arrival meet and transfer to the old city and start introductory city tour of Bukhara. Visit Samanid’s Mausoleum, Chashma Ayub, citadel Ark, Bolo-Hauz Mosque Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast full day city tour of Bukhara. Visit Samanid’s Mausoleum, Chashma Ayub, citadel Ark, Bolo-Hauz Mosque, Kalyan complex including Kalyan Mosque, Kalyan Minaret and Mirarab Medressah, Trade domes, Abdulazizkhan and Ulugbek Medressahs, Nadir Devonbegi Medressah, complex, Lyabi-Khauz complex. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast drive to Samarkand via Shakhrisabz. Shakhrisabz is birthplace of Great Amir Temur. Visit ruins of Ak Saray Palace, Dorut Saodat complex (Khazratiy Imam Mosque), Jahongir mausoleum, Dorut Tillovat complex: Gumbazi-Seyidon, Kok Gumbaz Mosque. Then continue driving to Samarkand. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast full day city tour of Samarkand. Visit Guri Emir Masoleum, Registan Square, Siyob bazaar, Bibihanym Mosque, Shahi Zinda Necropolis. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast continue city tour of Samarkand. Visit Ulugbek Observatory, Afrosiyob Museum and ruins. PM. Drive to Tashkent. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast full day city tour of Tashkent. Visit Khastimom complex (Kaffal Shashiy Masoleum, Barak Khan Medressah, Library with holy Koran), Kukaldosh Medressah, Chorsu Bazaar, Independence Square, Amir Timur Square, Navoi Theatre Square, some metro stations. Transfer to Tashkent International Airport for departure flight to Milan by HY-256 departing at 16:40.
