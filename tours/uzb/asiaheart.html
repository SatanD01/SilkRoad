Uzbekistan-Heart of Central Asia
Rating:
Duration: 11 days
Route: Tashkent-Khiva-Bukhara-Shakhrisabz-Samarkand
Uzbekistan-Heart of Central Asia
This 11 days tours allows the tourists to explore the most important touristic cities with wonderful monuments: Khiva, Bukhara, Samarkand, Shakhrisabz and Tashkent. Each of these cities is famous and rich in history, monuments and architecture. In 11 days you will receive the maximum interesting information from the excursions, and you will have enough free time that you can devote to the independent walks, unique photos and communication with people. For some of you such trip can be a return to the good old days when everyone lived in one big country, but for the younger generation, it will most likely be opening an entirely new world - a charming East!

Arrival of the clients at Tashkent International Airport by HY-232 flight from Frankfurt at 20:40. Meet and transfer to hotel. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast full day city tour of Tashkent. Visit Khastimom complex (Kaffal Shashiy Masoleum, Barak Khan Medressah, Library with holy Koran), Kukaldosh Medressah, Chorsu Bazaar, Independence Square, Amir Timur Square, Navoi Theatre Square, some metro stations. Take evening flight HY-1057 (18:40-20:20) to Urgench. Upon arrival meet and transfer to Khiva. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast full day city tour of Ichankala (inner town). Visit Kalta Minar, Muhammad Aminkhan Medressah, Kunya Ark, Muhammad Rakhimkhan Medressah, Juma Mosque and Minaret, Pahlavan Mahmud Masoleum, Islamkhodja Medressah, Toshhovli Palace, Allakulikhan Medressah and Ceravanserai. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast drive to Khiva via Kizilkum desert. En route photo stop at Amu Darya river bank. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast full day city tour of Bukhara. Visit Samanid’s Mausoleum, Chashma Ayub, citadel Ark, Bolo-Hauz Mosque, Kalyan complex including Kalyan Mosque, Kalyan Minaret and Mirarab Medressah, Trade domes, Abdulazizkhan and Ulugbek Medressahs, Nadir Devonbegi Medressah, complex, Lyabi-Khauz complex. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast continue exploring of Bukhara. Visit Sitorai Mohi Hosa- summer residence of Bukhara emirs, Chor Minor Mosque, Bahauddin Naqshbandiy Masoleum. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast drive to Samarkand via Shakhrisabz. Shakhrisabz is birthplace of Great Amir Temur. Visit ruins of Ak Saray Palace, Dorut Saodat complex (Khazratiy Imam Mosque), Jahongir mausoleum, Dorut Tillovat complex: Gumbazi-Seyidon, Kok Gumbaz Mosque. Then continue driving to Samarkand. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast full day city tour of Samarkand. Visit Guri Emir Masoleum, Registan Square, Siyob bazaar, Bibihanym Mosque, Shahi Zinda Necropolis. Overnight at Hotel.

After breakfast continue city tour of Samarkand. Visit Afrosiyob Museum and ruins, Ulugbek Observatory, St. Daniel’s Masoleum. Also visit Konigil ancient silk paper making center. Take evening Afrosiyob speedy train (17:00-19:10 or 18:00-20:10) to Tashkent. Overnight at Hotel.

Transfer to the International Airport for departure flight to Frankfurt by HY-231 departing at 05:35.
