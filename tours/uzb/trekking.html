Trekking/hiking tour in the villages (Nurata Mountains) 14 days
Rating:
Duration: 14 days
Route: Nurata Mountains
Trekking/hiking tour in villages (Nurata mountains) 14 days
Trekking/hiking tour in the Nurata mountains is a great rest after a working week or a good addition to a tour of Uzbekistan. You will see mountains, alpine meadows, mountain flowers, rivers and lakes, beautiful panoramas of mountain ranges, mountain villages, gardens, shepherds with their flocks of sheep, horses on free pastures, vineyards, mountain villagers and much more.
D-1 TASHKENT
Arrival of clients at Tashkent International Airport on a morning flight from their country of residence. Meeting and transfer to the hotel for luggage drop (check-in only after 14:00). Start of a full day tour of Tashkent. Visit the Khastim complex (Kaffal Shashiy Masoleum, Barak Khan Madrassah, Library with the Holy Quran), Chorsu Bazaar, Museum of Applied Arts, Independence Square, Amir Temur Square, several metro stations with its beautifulyour design. Overnight at the hotel.
Without food.
D-2 TASHKENT-URGENCH (by air)-KHIVA (30 km)
Transfer to domestic airport for morning flight HY-055 (07:40-09:20) to Urgench. Meeting and transfer to Khiva. Start of the day tour of Ichankala (inner city). Visit Kalta Minar, Muhammad Aminkhan Madrassah, Kunya Ark, Muhammad Rakhimkhan Madrassah, Juma Mosque and Minaret, Pahlavan Mahmud Mausoleum, Islamkhodzha Madrassah, Toshkhovli Palace, Allakulikhan Madrassah and Seravansarai. Overnight at the hotel.
Meals: breakfast at the hotel.
D-3 KHIVA - BUKHARA (480 km, 8-9 hours)
After breakfast transfer to Bukhara through the Kyzylkum desert. Demonstration of cooking pilaf in an Uzbek family. Overnight at the hotel.
Meals: breakfast at the hotel. Dinner with a local family.
D-4 BUKHARA
After breakfast, a sightseeing tour of Bukhara for the whole day. Visiting Samanid Mausoleum, Chashma Ayub, Ark Citadel, Bolo-Khauz Mosque, Kalyan Complex, including Kalyan Mosque, Kalyan Minaret and Mirarab Madrassah, Trading Domes, Abdulazizkhan and Ulugbek Madrasahs, Nadir Devonbegi Madrasah, complex, Lyabi-Khauz complex, Jewish synagogue. Folklore show at Nadir Devonbegi Madrasah (subject to availability). Overnight at the hotel.
Meals: breakfast at the hotel.
D-5 BUKHARA-GIDJDUVAN (65 km) –NURATA (120 km) –YANGIKAZGAN (70 km)
After breakfast, drive to Gijduvan and visit the family of ceramists. Then a trip to Navoi, on the way visiting the ruins of the Rabbat Malik caravanserai. Then a trip to Nurata, a small town near Aydarkul Lake. This place is famous for the holy spring "Chashmai Ayub". According to ancient legends, Alexander the Great built a small fortress to watch the enemy from the north side: here you can visit the ruins of two old castles of an unknown period. Also visit Nurata Mosque of the eighth century. Upon arrival in Yangikazgan, a short camel ride within the yurt camp (about 10 minutes). Overnight in yurts.
Meals: breakfast at the hotel. Dinner in yurts.
